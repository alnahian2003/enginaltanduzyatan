Selam! I'm

Engin Altan Düzyatan

My Beloved Fans Love to Call Me as Ertuğrul Bey

Who I Am?

I am a dreamy person, I love my work and family very much. I began my first acting in 2001 and since then I never had to look back.

Birth

I was born on 26 July 1979 in Karşıyaka, İzmir, Turkey.

Early life

I began acting during my high school days. My family were Turkish migrants from Yugoslavia. I joined Dokuz Eylül University to study theatre. Finally I completed the course and moved to İstanbul where I started my professional acting career.

Career

I made my screen debut in the television series "Ruhsar" and went on to appear in "Yeditepe İstanbul, Koçum Benim" and "Alacakaranlık". In 2005, I acted in the films Beyza'nın Kadınları directed by Mustafa Altıoklar and Kalbin Zamanı. In 2007, Düzyatan appeared in the series Sevgili Dünürüm with Haluk Bilginer, Sumru Yavrucuk and Nevra Serezli. His next roles were in the films Cennet (2008) and Mezuniyet (2009). In 2009, he played Doctor Serdar Batur in the series Bir Bulut Olsam with Melisa Sözen and Engin Akyürek, written by Meral Okay.

Personal life

On 28 August 2014, I get married with the granddaughter of Selim Soydan and Hülya Koçyiğit, Neslişah Alkoçlar. We have a son, Emir Aras (born 2016) and a daughter, Alara (born 2018).
